Cash Money: Overview

Cash Money offers lines of credit, auto equity loans, and payday loans from $1,500 to $10,000. Since 1992, Cash Money has been providing Canadians with fast access to short-term financing options. You’ll benefit from an easy application, more flexibility than banks, quick decision, and friendly customer service.

How will I receive my funds?

There are three ways you may receive your funds depending on how you applied for your loan.

  • Interac e-Transfer®
  • Direct deposit
  • Cash at cash-participating branch locations
